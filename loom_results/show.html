The "perfect play" benchmark. After each match we re-examine every decision against a near-optimal baseline: we run a quick Monte-Carlo equity estimate (the hand's chance to win vs a random range) and compare the model's action to correct pot-odds. A call/raise that's clearly -EV, or a fold of a clearly +EV spot, is flagged as a blunder. Higher % sound and lower blunders / bb-lost = closer to perfect.
